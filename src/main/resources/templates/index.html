Company Profile:

SGM Enterprise was established in 2023. SGM is a dynamic company offering integrated solutions in transport, manpower services, and training. With a steadfast commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we strive to deliver reliable, efficient, and innovative services that meet the diverse needs of our clients across various industries.


Mission Statement:

At SGM Enterprise, our mission is to provide high quality service along with fair and competitive pricing as well as exceptional transport, manpower and training solutions that empower individuals and businesses to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape, while upholding the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and excellence.


Transport Solutions:

Freight Transport: We specialize in the transportation of goods and cargo, offering comprehensive freight transport services tailored to the unique requirements of our clients. Our extensive fleet of vehicles and experienced drivers ensure timely and secure delivery of goods to destinations worldwide.

Parcel Delivery: SGM Enterprise provides efficient and cost-effective parcel delivery services for individuals and businesses. With flexible delivery options, we ensure that parcels reach their destinations safely and on time.

Specialized Transport: Our specialized transport services cater to the transportation needs of oversized, hazardous, and high-value cargo. Our team of experts is equipped to handle complex transportation challenges with precision and care.


Manpower Services

Recruitment and Placement: SGM Enterprise offers comprehensive recruitment and placement services to connect employers with qualified and skilled professionals across various industries and disciplines.

Staffing: We provide staffing solutions to help businesses meet short and long-term manpower needs, allowing them to maintain productivity and flexibility during normal days, peak periods, or special projects.

Outsourcing Solutions: Our outsourcing services enable businesses to streamline operations and reduce costs by outsourcing non-core functions such as administration, customer service, training, and logistics management etc.


Training Services:

Skills Development: SGM Enterprise offers a wide range of training programs and workshops designed to enhance the skills and competencies of individuals in various fields, including technical, soft skills, managerial, customer service, behavioural, logistics and warehouse management.

Module Creation: We provide professional module creation services (PPT module, e-Learning module) which play a crucial role in helping organizations and institutions to develop high-quality, effective learning materials to meet the needs of their audiences.

Corporate Training: Our corporate training solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs of organizations, addressing skill gaps and empowering employees to achieve peak performance and productivity.
